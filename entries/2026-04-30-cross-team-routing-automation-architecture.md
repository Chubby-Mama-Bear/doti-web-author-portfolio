# 2026-04-30 — Cross-Team Workflow Routing & Automation Architecture

## Phase  
Routing Architecture & Automation Readiness

## Summary

Expanded cross-team workflow design into structured routing architecture and automation feasibility planning.

This milestone focused on clarifying responsibility boundaries, designing routing logic options, evaluating automation platform capabilities, and introducing workflow status tracking to support reliable cross-team coordination.

---

## Key Actions Completed

### Clarified Responsibility Ownership

Confirmed that document accessibility formatting and validation responsibilities are handled by a designated validation team prior to publication.

This clarification established responsibility boundaries necessary for conditional routing logic design.

---

### Designed Multi-Option Routing Strategies

Developed three routing models to support cross-team submission handling:

1. Direct routing to validation team  
2. Routing through centralized intake workflow  
3. Conditional routing triggered when documents are included  

Evaluated flexibility and maintainability tradeoffs across models.

Conditional routing identified as highest adaptability design.

---

### Initiated Platform Capability Evaluation

Proposed structured comparison between:

- OpenForms  
- Microsoft Forms  

Objective:

Determine which platform supports conditional routing and cross-team workflow handling more effectively before committing to implementation.

---

### Investigated Power Automate Capability

Identified Power Automate as potential routing automation engine.

Confirmed:

- Platform availability exists  
- Licensing approval required  
- Formal IT request necessary  

Established pathway for automation access readiness.

---

### Maintained Supervisor Visibility

Provided proactive notification to supervisor outlining automation exploration intent prior to initiating IT access request.

Supervisor acknowledged direction approval.

Maintained governance transparency for system-level capability exploration.

---

### Standardized Output Format Expectations

Confirmed expected document output formats:

- Primary format: PDF  
- Secondary format: Word (as needed)

Established consistent publishing expectations to reduce workflow rework.

---

### Proposed Workflow Status Tracking Step

Introduced concept of validation confirmation checkpoint prior to final publication.

Objective:

Ensure document validation completion before publishing handoff.

Supports:

- Workflow reliability  
- Accountability  
- Cross-team visibility  

---

## Primary Outcome

Transitioned workflow development from governance alignment into structured routing architecture and automation readiness planning.

Established technical and governance foundation necessary for implementing conditional workflow execution.
